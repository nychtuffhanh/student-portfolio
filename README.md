## 📖 About the Project
This web application is a custom-designed, team-based portfolio that highlights the growth, achievements, and synergy of **Team Nexus**. Rather than presenting ourselves in isolation, we designed this unified space to show how our diverse skillsets—ranging from creative frontend layouts to complex backend algorithms—intersect.
This project is a custom-designed, collaborative website that functions as the digital home for **Team Nexus**, a group of three Computer Science students from Myanmar. 
It is structured to give recruiters, collaborators, and mentors an immediate, engaging overview of what we can achieve together as student developers.
Instead of hostings standalone resumes, we built this workspace to represent our unified skills and show how different coding disciplines interact. Visitors can explore our shared milestones, review our primary team project, inspect our technical capabilities, and seamlessly navigate to our individual developer profiles.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Modern Glassmorphism UI**: Built with a sleek, futuristic design system featuring smooth backdrops, translucent glass cards, and neon ambient glow elements.
- **Fully Responsive & Adaptive Layout**: Tailored to deliver a premium user experience across all devices—desktops, tablets, and mobile screens.
- **Dynamic Loader**: An elegant entry screen that welcomes users while resources load in the background.
- **Milestones & Education Timeline**: A visual representation of our collaborative and academic roadmap from 2023 to the present.
- **Unified Team Project Showcase**: Highlights our core software development projects, detailing the technologies used and key features.
- **Interactive Navigation**: Smooth scroll behaviors and responsive hamburger menu actions for mobile devices.
- **Futuristic Glassmorphic UI**: Translucent cards, neon accent drop-shadows, and smooth backdrop-filter effects that follow modern design guidelines.
- **Dynamic Loader**: A customized loading animation screen featuring the Team Nexus branding while project assets initialize.
- **Milestones & Education Timeline**: A structured timeline mapping out our academic progression and projects from 2023 to the present.
- **Interactive Filtering & Showcases**: A dedicated space highlighting key collaborative projects, listing full descriptions, specifications, and live demonstrations.
- **Fully Responsive Architecture**: Structured layout grids that adapt smoothly for desktops, tablets, and mobile browsers.
- **Smooth Navigation & Scroll**: Native smooth-scrolling with an active navigation link highlighting system.
